FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our meal delivery service.

How does the meal delivery process work?

Our meal delivery process is simple. You select your meals from our weekly menu, choose your delivery day, and we'll deliver your fresh, pre-portioned meals straight to your door in eco-friendly, insulated packaging that keeps everything at the proper temperature.

Are the meals fresh or frozen?

All our meals are prepared fresh using locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. They're delivered fresh, not frozen, and are designed to stay fresh in your refrigerator for up to 5 days.

How do I heat the meals?

Our meals come in microwave-safe containers and can be heated in 2-3 minutes. Detailed heating instructions are included with each delivery, and some meals can also be heated in a conventional oven if you prefer.

Can I customize my meals for dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer a variety of options for different dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, low-carb, and more. You can set your dietary preferences in your account, and our menu will filter to show you compatible meals.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time before your weekly cutoff date (typically 5 days before delivery). There are no cancellation fees or long-term commitments.
